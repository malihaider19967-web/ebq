YOAST SEO PREMIUM - FEATURES BY MENU ITEM (NON-AI)

[GENERAL]
- SEO Workouts: Access step-by-step routines (like the Orphaned Content or Cornerstone workouts) to improve site structure.
- Yoast Academy: Full access to all premium SEO training courses directly from the dashboard.
- 24/7 Support: Direct link to the premium support team for email or chat assistance.

[SETTINGS]
- Site Structure / Cornerstone Content: Mark and manage your most important articles for stricter optimization checks.
- Internal Linking: Toggle and configure the internal linking suggestion engine.

[REDIRECTIONS]

- CSV Export/Import: Bulk manage your redirects using spreadsheet files.

[POST/PAGE EDITOR - SEO SIDEBAR]

- Word Form Recognition: Analysis that understands plurals and different verb tenses of your keyword.
- Real-time Internal Linking Suggestions: A sidebar list of relevant internal posts to link to while writing.
- Social Tab: Manually set unique images, titles, and descriptions for social media platforms.

[EXTENSIONS / ADD-ONS]
- Local SEO: Manage business address, opening hours, and Google Maps schema.
- Video SEO: Configuration for video sitemaps and rich result appearance.
- News SEO: Settings for the News XML sitemap and Google News inclusion.
- WooCommerce SEO: Enhanced shop-specific SEO controls and breadcrumb settings.

[TOOLS]
- Orphaned Content Filter: Identify and fix posts that have no internal links pointing to them.
- File Editor: Edit your robots.txt and .htaccess files safely (Ads removed in Premium).
